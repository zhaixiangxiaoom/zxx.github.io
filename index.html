AI Form Filler - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Form Filler is a Chrome browser extension designed to help users automatically detect and fill form fields on web pages using AI-generated data. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

No personal data collection: AI Form Filler does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your browsing activity.

API key storage: Your Alibaba Cloud Bailian API key is stored locally in your browser's storage (Chrome sync storage) and is never sent to our servers or third parties.

Form data handling: When generating AI form data, only field metadata (field names, types, labels, placeholders) is sent to the AI service. Your actual form data remains entirely within your browser and is never transmitted.

3. Security Measures

All processing happens locally in your browser. No form data leaves your device during the filling process. The extension follows Chrome's security best practices and uses secure communication with AI services via HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension integrates with Alibaba Cloud Bailian AI services to generate form data. When you configure your API key, you are directly connecting to Alibaba Cloud's services. We do not control or manage these third-party services.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The latest version will always be available here. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
